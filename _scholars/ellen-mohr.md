Ellie Mohr is the 2025 recipient of the Judge Waddell Endowed Scholarship.

Her nominators described her as someone who "embodies what every coach wants in a player — her spirit, attitude, and leadership abilities made her a standout, a great teammate. I can think of no better person to teach or coach. She will have a significant impact and influence on the young lives she encounters in her career."

Ellie is further described as "always positive, excited, and optimistic — an ambitious self-starter with a wonderful sense of humor that perfectly balances her intellect, humility, and strong will to achieve. Her dynamic and charismatic personality is contagious. She loves to learn, loves to share, loves to support others, and never loses herself along the way."
