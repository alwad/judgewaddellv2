Abraham Martin Hils is the 2022 recipient of the Judge Waddell Endowed Scholarship.

Abe's nominators described him as "a truly special young man — a fantastic student who shows an incredible desire to learn. He is the type of student who can change the dynamic of a school. He speaks to everyone when he enters a room and thanks his teacher as he leaves. He has an uplifting effect on the morale inside our classrooms."

A second nominator wrote that Abe "genuinely cares about those around him. His effort and energy were infectious. He completes his work with excellence — a young man of the utmost integrity, character, and humility."
