Alyssa Nicole Harris is the 2023 recipient of the Judge Waddell Endowed Scholarship.

Those who nominated her described Alyssa as "a leader, compassionate, positive mindset, inclusive, role model, hard worker, devoted player, always willing to help."

She "pushes her teammates to be the best, but also knows the right words of encouragement — will be an outstanding teacher and coach one day. Her future students and players will love her."

Another nominator wrote that Alyssa "is one of the most admirable students I've ever encountered — she is the epitome of a young lady and a role model. She consistently goes above and beyond. Her compassion and empathy are visible in the community."
